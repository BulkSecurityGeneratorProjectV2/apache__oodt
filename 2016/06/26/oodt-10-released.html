Apache OODT 1.0 released

June 26, 2016

OODT 1.0!

We’ve finally done it. OODT 1.0 has been signed off and has hit the mirrors.

Thanks to everyone who has helped since OODT hit incubation at the Apache Software Foundation.

As ever, more details regarding build and deployment can be found over on https://oodt.apache.org/.

Great work, and here’s looking forward to some of the cool new stuff we have planned for 2.0!

You can read more about it on our blog here.
